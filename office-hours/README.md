# office-hours
Office Hours is a live & interactive webinar series presented by [Starburst DevRel](https://devcenter.starburst.io). The format is a 30 minute session where DevRel:

- delivers a technical demonstration of something for the first 20 minutes
- devotes the last 10 minutes to Q&A about the demo OR ANYTHING related to Trino and/or Starburst
